Water Quality Index (WQI)

The Water Quality Index is a tool developed by our team that provides a measure of harmful algal bloom risks in Clear Lake. This value was created through the analysis of historical water quality data, including turbidity, total dissolved solids, water temperature, pH, chlorophyll-a, and dissolved oxygen.
